How FlightDecide Helps

Every pilot asks the same question
before every flight. We help you answer it.

From student pilots to ATP holders, everyone needs a clear picture before departure. FlightDecide pulls together weather, performance, fuel, and weight data into one scored verdict.

8 Risk Categories

Weather, performance, fuel, weight and balance, airspace. Eight categories scored green, yellow, or red. See your risk at a glance before you ever touch the preflight checklist.

AI Powered Analysis

Our advanced AI engine reviews your complete flight picture and provides a detailed narrative briefing with specific mitigations. Not just data. Judgment, explained in plain language.

Extensive Aircraft Library

From trainers to jets. A growing library covering trainers, cross-country singles, twins, turboprops, and jets. Add your own aircraft by importing your POH performance data.

Real Weather Data

Live METARs, TAFs, winds aloft, G-AIRMETs, SIGMETs, and PIREPs fetched directly from aviationweather.gov. The same data your dispatcher uses.

Part 91, 135, and 121

VFR and IFR fuel planning with the correct reserve requirements for your operation type. Fly private or professionally. We have the right rules for your certificate.

Your Aircraft, Your Minimums

Set up your specific aircraft profile and personal weather minimums. Every analysis is tailored to you, your aircraft, and the way you fly.

Simple by design

A complete flight picture
in three steps.

No dispatcher, no lengthy briefing calls, no juggling six different apps. Just open FlightDecide and go.

Enter your route and aircraft

Set your departure, destination, and alternates. Choose from the built-in aircraft library or configure your own profile. Add passengers and fuel load.

We fetch weather, check NOTAMs, compute performance

FlightDecide pulls live METARs, TAFs, winds aloft, SIGMETs, and PIREPs. It computes weight and balance, takeoff and landing distances, and fuel burn using your aircraft's POH data.

Get your GO / CAUTION / NO GO verdict with AI briefing

Eight categories are scored and color-coded. Our AI engine synthesizes everything into a narrative briefing with specific risk callouts and mitigations. You make the final call, fully informed.
